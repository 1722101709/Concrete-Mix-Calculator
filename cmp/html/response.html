MIX CALCULATIONS
The mix calculations per unit volume of concrete shall be as follows: (a) Volume of concrete : 1m³ (b) Volume of cement : Mass of Cement Specific Gravity of Cement  *  1 1000  =  {{item8.cementContent}} {{item8.sgc}}  *  1 1000  =  {{item8.cementVolume}}m³ (c) Volume of water : Mass of water Specific Gravity of water  *  1 1000  =  {{item8.finalWater}} 1  *  1 1000  =  {{item8.waterVolume}}m³ (d) Volume of chemical admixture (superplasticizer) (@ 2.0 percent by mass of cementitious material) : Mass of chemical admixture Specific gravity of admixture  *  1 1000  =  {{item8.admixtureMass}} {{item8.sga}}  *  1 1000  =  {{item8.admixtureVolume}}m³ (e) Volume of all in aggregate : a - (b + c + d)  =  {1 - {{item8.cementVolume}} - {{item8.waterVolume}} - {{item8.admixtureVolume}}}  =  {{item8.volumeAll}}m³ (f) Mass of coarse aggregate : volume of all * Volume of coarse aggregate * Specific gravity of coarse aggregate * 1000 = {{item8.volumeAll}} * {{item8.finalVolumeOfCoarseAggregate}} * {{item8.sgca}} * 1000 = {{item8.massOfCoarseAggregate}}kg (f) Mass of fine aggregate : volume of all * Volume of fine aggregate * Specific gravity of fine aggregate * 1000 = {{item8.volumeAll}} * {{item8.finalVolumeOfFineAggregate}} * {{item8.sgfa}} * 1000 = {{item8.massOfFineAggregate}}kg
